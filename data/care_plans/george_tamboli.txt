RESPIRATORY MONITORING
----------------------
Check oxygen saturation with pulse oximeter at:
  - Morning: before activity
  - Afternoon: before outdoor walk
  - Night: before sleep

Target saturation: 88% – 92% (NOTE: higher targets may suppress respiratory drive in COPD)
If saturation falls below 85% and patient is breathless: sit patient upright,
call emergency services immediately, then contact supervising nurse.
