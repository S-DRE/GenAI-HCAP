DYSPHAGIA MANAGEMENT
--------------------
All fluids must be thickened to Level 2 (mildly thick) using prescribed thickener.
Do NOT give thin liquids (water, tea, juice) without thickener — aspiration risk.
Sit patient fully upright (90 degrees) during meals and for 30 minutes after.
Small bites only. Allow patient to fully swallow before next bite.
If patient coughs repeatedly during eating: stop feeding, sit upright, call nurse.

---

DEPRESSION MONITORING
---------------------
Patient has a history of low mood since stroke onset. Signs to report to nurse:
  - Refusing meals for more than one day
  - Refusing to get out of bed
  - Expressing hopelessness or tearfulness for more than 2 consecutive days
  - Any mention of self-harm

Encourage social interaction and praise all rehabilitation progress, however small.

---

PRESSURE SORE PREVENTION
-------------------------
Reposition patient every 2 hours when in bed.
Check skin at bony prominences (heels, hips, coccyx, shoulder blades) daily.
Report any redness that does not fade within 30 minutes to supervising nurse.
