BLOOD GLUCOSE TARGETS
---------------------
Fasting (morning, before breakfast): 4.0 – 7.0 mmol/L
Post-meal (2 hours after eating): below 10.0 mmol/L
If reading is above 15.0 mmol/L: contact supervising nurse immediately.
If reading is below 3.5 mmol/L: administer fast-acting glucose (orange juice or glucose gel)
and contact supervising nurse within 15 minutes.
